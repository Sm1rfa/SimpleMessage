SimpleMessage

Version:1.10.1
Developed by:Stoyan Bonchev https://sbonchev.eu
Contact:contact@sbonchev.eu

SimpleMessage is container for messaging applications, using their web-based chat options. In example you can login and use Google Hangouts, Facebook Messenger, Slack and others. The application is developed by Electron JS

SimpleMessage is completely free for use and distribution.
